Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical paving project take? ‚ñº

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard driveway typically takes 3-5 days, while larger areas or complex patterns may take 1-2 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the consultation.

What is concrete stamping? ‚ñº

Concrete stamping is a technique where patterns are pressed into freshly poured concrete to create textures that resemble natural materials like stone, brick, slate, or wood. It's a durable and cost-effective alternative to natural stone paving.

Can you work with my design ideas? ‚ñº

Absolutely! We welcome client input and can work with your design ideas or provide our own suggestions based on your property and preferences. Our team will ensure the final design is both aesthetically pleasing and functional.

Do you handle permits and approvals? ‚ñº

Yes, we manage all necessary permits and approvals for your project. Our team is familiar with local regulations and will ensure your project complies with all requirements. This service is included in our project management fee.
